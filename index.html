Features


Performance that goes the distance


They say life is full of compromises. That you can’t have high performance and long battery life on the same laptop. That a high-performance gaming laptop has to be big, bulky, and heavy. Well, the IdeaPad 3 Gen 6 (15" AMD) laptop proves them wrong. Now you can enjoy elite gaming performance on a thin and light device with amazing battery life, thanks to the new AMD Ryzen™ 5000 H-Series Mobile Processors. Never compromise on your laptop again.



Cool under pressure

The IdeaPad 3 Gen 6 (15" AMD) stays cool and quiet with mechanical airflow enhancements and intelligent thermals that ensure air flows out without crossing over intake vents. Calibrate performance and cooling even further with Q control by cycling through three modes of fan speeds to match your performance needs.











Full-time privacy control


Take complete control of your privacy with the IdeaPad 3 Gen 6 (15" AMD) laptop. Worries of being watched by hackers or still on-screen after a video call are gone with this low-tech lifesaver that lets you physically block your webcam lens.



Always-on digital wellness


Remove any concerns over eye strain, or household distractions interrupting important calls. The IdeaPad 3 Gen 6 (15" AMD). Smart learning solutions such as Eye Care, to reduce harmful low blue light, and Noise Cancelling that blocks out background noises make this the ideal laptop to lounge and learn on.







See more of everything


The IdeaPad 3 Gen 6 (15" AMD) laptop boasts four-sided narrow bezels on its 15.6″ FHD display. This translates to one of the highest active area ratios of its class, so you can take in wider viewing angles with less clutter.
